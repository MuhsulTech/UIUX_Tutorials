Build Your Professional UI/UX Portfolio

This module helps you create real UI/UX case studies and projects that demonstrate your skills to clients, recruiters, and companies.

Case Studies Portfolio Real Projects

1. What Are Portfolio Projects?

Portfolio projects are real or simulated UI/UX projects that show your process, thinking, and final results. They are the most important part of your design career.

Why they matter
  • ◎ Show your skills in action
  • ◎ Demonstrate your process
  • ◎ Build trust with clients
  • ◎ Stand out from other designers
What they include
  • ◆ Research
  • ◆ Wireframes
  • ◆ UI design
  • ◆ Prototype

2. Why Case Studies Matter

Case studies show how you think, not just what you designed. Companies hire designers for their process, not only their visuals.

Case studies reveal
  • ◎ Problem‑solving ability
  • ◎ UX thinking
  • ◎ Design decisions
  • ◎ Real results
What companies look for
  • ◆ Clear process
  • ◆ Clean UI
  • ◆ Usability
  • ◆ Impact

3. Recommended Portfolio Projects

These projects cover all major UI/UX skills and are perfect for your portfolio.

1. Portfolio Website

A complete personal website with homepage, projects, about, and contact pages.

2. E‑commerce UI/UX Case Study

Design a shopping experience: product listing, product page, cart, and checkout.

3. Dashboard UI/UX

Create a data dashboard with charts, filters, and user controls.

4. Mobile App UI/UX

Design a mobile app with onboarding, home screen, and core features.

4. Case Study Structure

Every case study should follow a clear structure that shows your thinking and results.

Case study outline
  • ◎ Problem statement
  • ◎ Research
  • ◎ Personas
  • ◎ User flows
  • ◎ Wireframes
  • ◎ UI design
  • ◎ Prototype
  • ◎ Final results
Tips for strong case studies
  • ◆ Show your process
  • ◆ Explain decisions
  • ◆ Use visuals
  • ◆ Keep it simple

5. Portfolio Tips

Your portfolio is your most important asset. Make it clean, simple, and focused.

Do this
  • ◎ Use clean layouts
  • ◎ Show 2–4 strong projects
  • ◎ Write clear case studies
  • ◎ Add a simple contact form
Avoid this
  • ◆ Too many projects
  • ◆ No explanation of process
  • ◆ Overly complex UI
  • ◆ Broken links or prototypes

6. Final Project: Create Your Portfolio

This is your final assignment — build your complete UI/UX portfolio website.

🏆
Final Project
  1. Choose 2–4 projects from this course.
  2. Create full case studies for each one.
  3. Design your portfolio website in Figma.
  4. Build it using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  5. Publish it online (GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel).
